My Portfolio

"There is something about building and creating;
watching all the pieces fall into place with a sense of pride in seeing hours of work come to life"

It started with an old Lord of the Rings RTS game and wanting so badly to insert my own creative changes into the game. After searching hours for game mods made by others I thought, “if others have done it, why not me?” And that is just what I did. I dove straight into the code and, though I got lost several times, I made my first new character in the game. A sense of achievement washed over me and I knew I wanted more. Several characters and a college degree later, I still feel satsfied when a project is completed.

There is something about building and creating; watching all the pieces fall into place with a sense of pride in seeing hours of work come to life. Whether it’s organizing and tinkering with spreadsheet data or thinking of new ways to improve an app I just built, I can’t help getting immersed in the process. Every new or revised project is a chance to learn and there you will find me deep in the middle of it.
